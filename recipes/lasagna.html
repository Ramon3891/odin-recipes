Lasagna

What a lasagna is

Lasagna is a typical Italian dish that has several regional variants throughout the country.

Ingredients for 4/6 people

To prepare a good lasagna, you need to make your own dough and meat sauce.
Here are the ingredients for the dough:

  • 3 eggs
  • 300g flour
  • salt

Here are the ingredients for the meat sauce:

  • 700g ground meat (beef and pork)
  • 70g celery
  • 100g carrots
  • 180g onion
  • 400g tomato sauce
  • A sprig of rosemary
  • A glass of white wine
  • 20g olive oil
  • Pepper and salt
